Features

Everything Sony refused to build.

Imaging Edge crashes on launch. PC Remote hasn't been updated since the Jurassic period. We started from scratch. With spite. It helps.

📡

Live Camera Preview

Real-time WiFi preview from your ILCE-7C. No lag. No cables hanging off your laptop like a sad octopus. Just your camera, your network, and a full-res feed on any of your screens.

Voice Commands

"Shoot." "Focus." "Burst five." "ISO auto." Your camera listens now. Sony's app never did. Built with on-device speech recognition — no cloud, no subscription, no drama.

On-device No cloud
🫰

Tongue Snap Shutter

Snap your tongue against the roof of your mouth. The camera fires. 🫰→📸

Yes, it's real. Yes, it works. No, we don't know why we built this. Actually, yes we do: because we could.

Proprietary tech
🌈

Nanoleaf + AI Mood Lighting

Connect your Nanoleaf panels. Point an AI at them. Say "dramatic sunset portrait vibe." Watch your studio transform. Also supports auto-matching lights to on-screen content, rainbow vomit mode (P11), and a surprisingly functional REST client.

REST API AI 🌈 vomit

🦫 Beaver Watermark

Every photo in the free tier features a tasteful, artisanal beaver emoji in the corner. Pixel-perfect. Centered. Unavoidable.

Your friends will ask about it. "What's that beaver on your photo?" "Oh that's just my camera app." You'll explain. They'll download it. The beaver is viral marketing.

Apple Watch Companion

Live preview on your wrist. Tap the Digital Crown to shoot. Because sometimes your hands are full, your phone is charging, and you still need to nail the shot. Also it looks incredibly cool at parties.
